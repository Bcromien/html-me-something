Slouchy Hat | Knitting Pattern

by Bridget Cromien

Who doesn't love the perfect slouchy hat for a bad hair day?! This simple pattern is beginner-friendly, knit in the round and slightly textured with a broken rib stitch. No matter how many hats I make, I just keep coming back to this old favorite. :)

Materials Needed

  • 1 skein of worsted weight yarn
  • US size 4 circular needles
  • US size 5 circular needles
  • Stitch markers

Gauge

10 stitches of 10 rows should result in 2x2'' swatch. If your gauge is off, adjust needle size to ensure fit.

Pattern

Band

Cast on 44 stitches.
K1 P1 until the end of the row and place marker.
K1 P1 until work measures 2 inches long.

Body

Switch to larger needles.
Row 1: Knit all stitches.
Row 2: K1 P1
Repeat the last 2 rows until piece measures 6in long
(Knit it longer if you want more slouch or shorter if you want it more fitted).

Decrease for the Top

Place stitch marker after every 8 stitches.
Row 1: Knit to 1 stitch before the marker and K2.
Row 2: K1 P1
When only 5 stitches remain, use tapestry needle to close and tie off.
